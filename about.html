About Automation Business Forms

Over six decades of printing excellence in Zimbabwe

1960s

Company Founded

Automation Business Forms was established, becoming a well-known name in Zimbabwe's printing industry.

2000

Acquisition by The Feathered Quill

The Feathered Quill (Private) Limited took over ABF from Commercial and Industrial Holdings following restructuring.

Present

Industry Leader

One of Zimbabwe's largest printing operations, serving over 300 customers nationwide with modern equipment and dedicated service.

Our Vision

We envision becoming the nation's largest printing company. We are currently looking at expansion possibilities particularly with regards establishing or acquiring a production facility in Harare. We would also like to expand our product mix to incorporate other print related products and acquire ISO certification.

Our Mission

We are in a service industry and therefore SERVICE is our prime consideration. Our business is to produce a wide range of printed matter as required by an expanding net of customers at a good price, of appropriate quality and delivered when needed.
